Welcome to TaskFlow

TaskFlow is your personal to-do manager designed to help you stay organized, boost productivity, and never miss a deadline again. Whether you're a student, professional, or homemaker, TaskFlow makes it easy to manage your daily tasks.

How to Use TaskFlow

  1. Register and login to your account.
  2. Add tasks with titles, deadlines, and priorities.
  3. Edit or delete tasks as your schedule changes.
  4. Check off completed tasks to stay motivated.
